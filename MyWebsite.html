Synthesizers are Cool

Source: Perfect Circuit

These are common instruments in today's music. You've always heard them in Electronic Dance Music or in the latest Pop single to hit radio or the net. Synthesizers are one of the most unique instruments and an inspiration to how it is applied in Music Production.

They can be as simple as mere keyboard with knobs and levers and as complex as having them connected to soundboards and modulators which broaden the possibilities of creating a unique sound. The essense of creating your own sound and expressing it through the keyboard is what I love about them.

Why are they cool?

Synthesizers are used primarily to:

  • create a melody
  • harmonize or combine with melody
  • function as an all-in-one instrument
